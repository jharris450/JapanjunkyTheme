JapanJunky Screensaver Preview

This is a standalone preview of the Three.js lacquer screensaver background. Move your mouse to see the parallax effect and cursor reaction on floating objects.

What You're Seeing

Low-poly Mt. Fuji landscape rendered at 320x240 with PS1-style vertex snapping, quantized to the VGA 256-color palette with Floyd-Steinberg dithering, nearest-neighbor upscaled. The camera orbits slowly (~100s per revolution).

Scene Elements

Mt. Fuji (dark silhouette) + snow cap + foothills, wireframe ocean with sine waves, 3 low-poly trees, 8 floating geometric primitives (icosahedrons, octahedrons, tetrahedrons), 3 particle lattice clusters, and 2 rotating wireframe shapes (sphere + torus).
